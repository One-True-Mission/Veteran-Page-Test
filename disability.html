Disability Resources for Veterans

Straightforward links and guidance to help you with VA disability claims, ratings, appeals, and programs that support daily life.

What Counts as a Service-Connected Disability

A condition that was caused or made worse by your military service. Examples include physical injuries, hearing loss, TBI, PTSD, and chronic illnesses.

  • VA: Eligibility overview

How to Apply for VA Disability Benefits

  1. Gather service records and medical evidence.
  2. File online through VA.gov/disability or work with a VSO.
  3. Attend any requested C&P exam.
  4. Track your claim and review your decision letter.
